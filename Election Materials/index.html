About Me

My name is Prayag Adhikari, and I'm a freshman majoring in Computer Science. I am excited to announce my candidancy for Student Senator for the class of 2028.

My goal is to build strong connections between students, faculty, and administration for our academic and social growth. I'm excited to work with the Student Government Association and create engaging events that enhance our campus spirit and support our new student body.

I look forward to the opportunity to serve you and contribute to positive change at Caldwell University.

Vision

Empowering Student Voices

I envision Caldwell University where every student feels empowered to share their ideas and concerns. As your Student Senator, I will create platforms for open speech, ensuring that all voices are represented.

Strengthening Community Connections

I aim to strengthen the connections between students, faculty, and administration, promoting a collaborative environment. We can enhance our campus experience by facilitating meaningful partnerships and communication.

Enriching Campus Life

I aim to enrich campus life through engaging events that promote unity. By organizing various activities for our student body, we can create a vibrant community where everyone feels included and valued.
